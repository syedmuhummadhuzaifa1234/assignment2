HYDREBAD:

Hyderabad is a major city located in the Sindh province of Pakistan.
It is the second-largest city in the province after Karachi and the sixth-largest city in Pakistan.
Situated along the eastern bank of the Indus River,
Hyderabad serves as an important economic, cultural, and historical center in the region.
The city of Hyderabad has a rich history that dates back centuries.
It was founded in 1768 by Mian Ghulam Shah Kalhoro,
who named it after the famous city of Hyderabad in southern India. 
Over the years, Hyderabad has witnessed the rule of various dynasties,
including the Talpurs, the Kalhoras, and the British.
